Beyond repetitive workflows.

The workflows above run continuously. Three more categories often round out a full project.

Always-on

Custom dashboards

Read-only operator views built on top of your existing tech stack.

  • Pull live data from HubSpot, Notion, Airtable, Slack, ERP, or any tool with an API
  • Unified pipeline view, ops dashboard, exec snapshot, custom KPIs
  • Built to your team's logic, not a generic BI template
  • Different from the Agent Ops Center: this views your business data, not your agents
Project-based

Project-based agents

One-time or low-frequency builds that compress a week of manual work into hours.

  • Bespoke scraping or data collection (e.g. directory builds, market mapping)
  • Quarterly research deliverables for your clients
  • Migration agents, audit agents, one-off enrichment runs
  • Built once, re-runnable when needed, scoped tight
Foundational

Build your brain

A structured knowledge base your team and your agents can both query.

  • Obsidian or GitHub-based second brain, structured for retrieval
  • Captures playbooks, SOPs, decisions, and tribal knowledge in one place
  • Becomes the source of truth that every agent reads from
  • Owned by your team, version-controlled, no vendor lock-in

Process

How a project actually runs.

Predictable rhythm. Clear handoffs. No surprises in week six.

i

Intro call

30-min scoping conversation. Honest read on fit, tier, and whether automation is even the right answer.

ii

Kickoff

Workshop to map the workflow, agree on outcomes, set the success metric.

iii

Requirements

Interviews with operators and stakeholders. Edge cases surfaced before code.

iv

Build & ship

Iterative build with weekly demos. Deploy to your environment.

v

Testing & QA

Structured testing across edge cases, guardrail checks, and review with your team.

vi

Iterations

Refine prompts, logic, and tools based on real-world runs before final handover.
